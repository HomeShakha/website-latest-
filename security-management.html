How it Works

HomeShakha's Security Management module simplifies communication between residents and the main gate. It enables residents to notify guards that they are expecting a visitor or delivery and guards to seek the permission of a resident when someone comes unannounced. This seemingly simple connection can bring powerful change to any community.
