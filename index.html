What is HomeShakha ?

An app that simplifies life for everyone in a gated community, from residents and management committee members to security guards and facility managers. Packed with features, it reduces many hassles—authorising entry of delivery executives, paying maintenance bills, raising a ticket to the facility manager—to a single click.

Security Management

Ensure that every person, package or vehicle entering the community is authorised by a resident. Effective security measures can be convenient, too!.

Know More

Community Management

Manage accounts and payments, resolve complaints and keep the community up-to-date with all that’s going on in the society. Easy for the management committee and residents.
