Accounts & Payments

The Smart way to manage your Society's finances.

Place the burden of maintaining your society’s books on the trusted shoulders of HomeShakha’s accounting platform, which has all the features necessary to improve processes and ease the work of the management committee.

SCHEDULE DEMO

Features of Accounts & Payments

Comprehensive accounting toolkit

Managing your accounts will never have been easier. Multiple options for invoice generation, ability to capture various payment types, real-time reports and ability to make online payments.

Maintenance payments

Your residents will appreciate our low payment charges and the multitude of payment options. Any credit or debit card, netbanking, UPI and even a few wallets are accepted. Expect payment collection to be quicker and smoother.

Real-time reports

Residents can view/download all their receipts and admins can quickly generate reports for payments and dues (by account, payment mode or date) and even compile reconciliation reports.
